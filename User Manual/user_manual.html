User Manual

General

1. Install the WE INDIA study app from play store.
2. Open the app and enter the credentials such as name, email id and mobile number.
3. verify through OTP, which you would receive through SMS.
4. Open your desired exam(ex: TNPSC GROUP 2)
5. A list of subjects with free sample access for each subject will be available.
6. Open the sample subject, it will start downloading the entire deck containing cards of that particular subject.
7. At first, the topic of the card will be shown. Please try to recollect the facts related to the topic and click on the show answer button.
8. Now the facts and answers of the topic will be displayed along with images and audio.
9. At the bottom 3 buttons, HARD MEDIUM and EASY with various time options will available. By default, it will have 5mins, 1hour and 2days, which can be altered according to user preferences in settings.
10. If you find a topic hard to remember, click on HARD. Which will loop the cards and show it to you again after 5 mins.if clicked on MEDIUM the cards will be looped for 1 hour and will be shown to you after 1 hour. When clicked on EASY, cards will be shown in 2days again. This way the revision will be very effective as you can balance between hard medium and easy topics and can recall instinctively during exams. The cards can be looped any number of times.
11. Once you check the free sample decks.The aspirants can go to a particular subject and buy it with all contents at a very low cost which will be 5RS,10RS or 20RS for each subject.
12. Once you click on your subject, which you are desired to buy. The app will forward you to a payment gateway having multiple payment options such as google pay, patym, debit card, net banking, and other payment options.
13. After the payment is done you will receive an invoice through SMS and that particular subject cards will start downloading. Please bear with us as each deck of cards will be 50 to 60 Mb and may take 30 secs to 2 mins depending on your internet speed.
14. Now the cards will be accessible to you for 6 months and we will keep adding the lastest topics and will be automatically added to your deck without any charges till your validity expires.
15. As the users are going through the cards and find any mistakes or typo errors .please feel free to give us feedback through the feedback option on the menu.
16. If users want a specific topic and are missing in the subject deck. The users can send us feedback and we will add the requested topic in a short while.
17. If users want to reschedule the cards as present when newly purchased.Can do so using settings-Reset cards.
18. Card browser options are available in the menu for searching particular cards and related facts.
19. User statistics can be viewed from the menu-statistics option.
20. Decks option in the menu will show all decks of exams and subjects.

Options available for users

1. Users can add new cards to the subjects which they have purchased.
2. Users can create a new subject for free and add their notes or copy-paste from online articles directly.
3. User added cards can contain texts, images, and audios.
4. Users can add extra notes to cards already present from the edit note option available in the top right corner.
5. Picture of newspaper articles can be added instantaneously using camera option while adding cards.
6. Personal audios or news article audios can be directly recorded and added to the cards.
7. Pictures can be zoomed in and out.
